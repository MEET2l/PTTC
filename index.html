PTTC Program Overview

Our Preschool Teacher Training Course equips educators with the skills needed to bring activity-based learning and joyful teaching into classrooms.

Who Can Join?

Preschool teachers, aspiring educators, and early childhood professionals.

Duration

3 Months (Flexible schedule with weekly modules).

Mode of Training

Online, In-person, or Hybrid training sessions.

Key Benefits

Certification, practical classroom skills, activity-based teaching tools.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our PTTC Program

Do I need prior teaching experience?

No prior teaching experience is required. The PTTC program is designed for beginners as well as existing preschool teachers.

Is the PTTC program online or offline?

The program is available in both online and in-person modes, depending on your location and preference.

What is the duration of the PTTC course?

The PTTC program runs for 3 months, including training sessions, practical learning, and assessments.

What certification will I receive?

Upon successful completion, you will receive a Kreedo PTTC Certification that supports your professional growth.

Will I get teaching materials during the course?

Yes, participants get access to structured curriculum resources and activity-based learning materials.
